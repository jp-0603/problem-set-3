On this website, we are only going to focus on the Warriors and Rockets(the two first teams on the Western Conference)and the Raptors and Celtics(the first two teams on the Eastern Conference).
The Warriors have had MUCH success in the past years. The Warriors starting lineup is pretty good as they won three championships in the last 4 years. Their point guard is a three-point specialist and even some people call him the greatest shooter in the NBA. He is also known for his clutch factor. His most famous clutch shot was against the Thunder on Februray 28, 2016 where he hit a three pointer from 38 feet. He is also a two-time MVP, which one of them was unananimous. This Klay Thompson is a very accurate three point shooter. He works very well off the ball, working off of screens and plays. He also complements Stephen Curry. The Thunder were leading the series 3-1 and the Warriors went on a streak to win three games in a row. Kevin Durant signed with the Warriors after the season ended.
